Baklava


Ingredients

  • 1 Package of Filo Dough
  • 1.5 cups Butter
  • 2 cups finely chopped nuts of your choosing
  • 3/4 cup of water
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 Tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 tsp ginger powder

Directions

  1. Read all directions before begining.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  3. Butter chosen highwall square pan.
  4. Toss nuts with cinnamon ginger powder and half of the brown sugar
  5. Unroll Filo dough, cut whole stach to fit pan, cover with damp cloth.
  6. Gently lay Filo dough in pan, 2 sheets at a time, Painting generously with butter.
  7. Count sheets as you lay them down, adding a thin, covering layer of the nut mixture every 10 sheets.
  8. Finish the stack with a minnimum of 8 sheets of filo dough with no nut layer.
  9. Cuttting all the way to the bottom of the pan, create desired size and pattern of pieces.
  10. Bake for 45 minutes or until top sheets are golden in color
  11. While pan is baking, make sauce by simmering all additional ingredients for about 20 minutes.
  12. Pour thickened sauce over baklava.
  13. Let cool, and Enjoy!
