About the Project

I chose to create a page aobut fire because the art of making a smokeless fire is drifting into the past.

In todays changing climate, where forest fires are increasingly common, it is essential for us to know how to control fire. In order to control fire, we must understand how fire works.

As a professional in the outdoor youth develoment industry I spent many long days and nights attempting to instill a sense of responsibility for our environment and planet in the youth that I served. Due to a lack of societal monetary investment in these "on the ground" skills, I have turned to digital media to sustain my family's needs and communicate these values to a generation that is increasingly online.
